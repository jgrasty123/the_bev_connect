FAQ

Questions, answered.

Where do you ship?

We ship alcohol compliantly to every U.S. state except Utah and Alabama (42+ states), and non-alcoholic packages to all U.S. states, Armed Services addresses, Canada and Mexico.

Is this month-to-month?

Yes. Our programs are month-to-month — your success is our success, and we earn the relationship every month.

What carriers do you use?

UPS and GLS (where applicable), with steep industry shipping discounts. Options include Overnight, 2-Day, 3-Day and Ground.

Can you engrave bottles and glassware?

Yes — bottles, glassware and plaques. We also handle special messages, greeting cards, gift boxes, baskets and crates.

Do I have to use your marketing services?

Not at all. The 3PL fulfillment stands entirely on its own. Marketing is an optional add-on if you want growth support under the same roof.
